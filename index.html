Our journey

Experience the rich flavors of original kinalas at MingTeng's, where our culinary journey began with a passion for serving others. Rooted in Bikolnon traditions, our origin story is a tale of family recipe, local ingredients, and a commitment to crafting a memorable dining experience for Bikolnons. From humble beginnings to a thriving establishment, we've woven a narrative of culinary excellence that celebrates the heart and soul of our community.
